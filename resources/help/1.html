What is TODO-APP ?
Is a tool for creating and saving Tasks or ToDos
TODO-APP Features
  • Cross platform - Works in Major Operating Systems (Linux, Windows, MacOS)
  • Concise and simple
  • Tray support (When closing the App it minimizes in Tray area)
  • Soft Delete of ToDos (This means when you delete something, you'l only loose access to it, but it will stay in the database)
  • Three states of ToDos (Pending, Doing, Done)
  • Portable App (No need to install any databases)
  • Multy List Creation
